Background

Location:

Central Europe, between Switzerland and Austria

Land:

Lichtenstien is diveded into two regions with the Oberland in the south having mountainous terrain and the countries capital Vaduz while the Unterland in the north is more flat.

Economy:

Liechtenstein is a prosperous, highly industrialized, and diversified free-enterprise economy. With its top three imports being agricultural products, raw materials, and energy products. Top 3 exports being small specialty machinery, connectors for audio and video, parts for motor vehicles, dental products, and hardware. With its top 3 importers being Germany, Austria, and Switzerland. Lastly the percentage of people under the poverty line is 0.1% to 3.1%.
