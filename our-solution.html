Our Solution

High living costs

The current government could change their taxing policy to have a more equitable progressive tax system. Where the richer you are the more money you have to give to the government in taxes. These taxes could be used to help pay for the common people’s human rights such as food, water and shelter.

Gender Inequality

More people will have money and will be less poor. Usually in more wealthy countries protests start to rise up and the now more empowered people can change government policies to be less sexist.

Low Job Opportunities

The influx of money from the new taxing policy would support smaller businesses encouraging people to start their own small businesses creating more jobs.
