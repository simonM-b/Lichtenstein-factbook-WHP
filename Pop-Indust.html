Population

After examining my country's population pyramid I'v noticed that the population pyramid looks like a hill or a wave. As the age goes up the percentage of people in that age group increases. However as you continue up the pyramid the population percentage goes back down. We can see that their population has continued to grow and as the industrial revolution started, they transferred from farming to factory working. And finally I predict that the population of Liechtenstein will continue to grow while the population percentage of middle aged people continues to be the largest.
